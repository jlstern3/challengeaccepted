Welcome to Challenge Accepted!

They say humans are creatures of habit, so why not start a habit of caring for yourself through daily exercise — but the easy way? Whether you’re an avid athlete or someone looking to ease back into regular movement, Challenge Accepted is here to support you all the way.

Stay active for a month straight, working day-by-day in manageable increments towards an ultimate fitness goal. We’ll provide fresh monthly challenges to keep you intrigued and appropriately challenged, all you have to do is commit and trust the process! Are you ready to accept our challenge?!
