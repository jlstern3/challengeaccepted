Welcome to Challenge Accepted!

Challenge Accepted makes it easy for users to stay active for 30-days straight, working day-by-day towards an ultimate fitness goal. The app will track the user's progress within their selected challenge that month and also track the user's streak of back-to-back monthly challenges completed. Users can connect with friends and share in the fun together.

Everyone from serious athletes to inactive individuals looking to ease their way back into exercising. Challenges will require no equipment and are not age specific.
